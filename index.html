FORMULA 1
Formula 1 is the premier international single-seater motorsport championship, showcasing the worlds most advanced racing cars and elite drivers. Known for its high-speed races on iconic tracks across the globe, Formula 1 combines cutting-edge technology, precision engineering, and immense strategic planning. Teams such as Ferrari, Mercedes, and Red Bull compete in a series of races called Grands Prix, aiming to secure both the Drivers' and Constructors' Championships.
